RESEARCH PROJECTS

  • The design of Band-gap voltage generation

    • Output voltage less than ±1% variation and less than ±5% variation in PVT simulation

  • A 10b 11.3MS/s SAR ADC Achieving 57dB SINAD and 9.16 ENOB in tsmc0.18um

    • Responsible for design of logic and DAC, joint debugging and MATLAB calculating

    Selected monotonic switching procedure, realized good FOM and high linearity

  • The design of a Programmable Gain Amplifier

    • Considered gain variation, in-band gain ripple, noise, diff PM, 3dB-BW, THD and OIP3

    Completed PVT simulation in tsmc0.18um, met all specifications

  • Realization of Transmitter: Voltage Control Oscillator & Power Amplifier

    • Realized LCVCO and two-stages class-A PA, 2FSK modulation, 20dBm output power

    Achieved high 1dB compression point and finished impedance matching

  • Low-power SAR-ADC modeling using MATLAB

    • Compared 9 DAC structures and analyzed the power consumption of each

    Proposed High solution ADC algorithm and new switching scheme

  • An Inductor-Less Noise-Cancelling Broadband LNA (470-870MHz)

    • Practiced 4 methods to simulate NF and two fast algorithms to simulate IIP3

    Finished with the S21>15dB, NF<2.5dB and a constrained current of 5mA in tsmc0.18um

  • A quadrate-coupled and filter-enhanced low-phase-noise Ring VCO

    • Designed a 0.6-3 GHz phase-coupled low-noise ring VCO in 28nm SMIC CMOS process using low pass filter

    Achieved a phase noise of -126.5 dBc/Hz at 10MHz offset from the carrier at 1.5GHz
